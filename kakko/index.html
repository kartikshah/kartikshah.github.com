Kakko is a gujarati keyboard. Now you can use gujarati in any app across iOS platform. Just setup custom keyboard from the settings app on iOS devices and start typing in gujarati.

Check out how to Setup keyboard on your iPhone and iPad.

Keyboard supports both dark and light theme depending on the surrounding application.

Head over to Help section for a quick tutorial on how to write half letters and special characters.
