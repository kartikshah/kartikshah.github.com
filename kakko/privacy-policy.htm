Kakko - Gujarati Keyboard Privacy Policy

Overview

Your privacy is very important to us. In this privacy policy, we let you know what information we collect, why we collect it and how we use it. By using the keyboard app, you consent to this privacy policy.

No Collection of information that you type

When you enable Kakko - Gujarati Keyboard in your iOS settings, the app does not collect any personal information or upload any such information to any server.

When you enable full access in the setting app, you will see a warning message stating "full access allows the developer to transmit anything you type, including things you have previously typed with this keyboard. This could include sensitive information such as your credit card number or street address" Kakko - Gujarati Keyboard is NOT designed to receive, store or transmit any such sensitive data.

When you send use feedback via email, your email address will be visible to us after you feedback is sent. We may use your email address to contact you to provide technical support. 

Disclosure of personal information

We will not disclose your personal information to any third party unless you have consented to such disclosure or when we are required to do so by law.

Policy Changes

We will post changes to this policy on the website. Please check our websites regularly to view our up to date privacy policy.
